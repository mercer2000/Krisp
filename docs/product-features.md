# MyOpenBrain — Product Features

*Your AI-powered second brain for work. Capture everything, forget nothing.*

---

## Email Command Center

Connect multiple Gmail and Outlook accounts into a unified inbox. AI automatically classifies incoming emails with smart labels, detects newsletters and spam, extracts action items, and routes messages to the right workspace pages — all without you lifting a finger.

- **Unified multi-account inbox** — Gmail and Outlook side by side
- **AI-powered classification** — Smart labels auto-tag emails by custom rules you define in plain English
- **Action item extraction** — AI scans emails for tasks assigned to you and creates Kanban cards automatically
- **Auto-draft replies** — Generate context-aware draft responses for labeled emails
- **VIP contacts** — Flag important senders for priority visibility
- **Newsletter detection** — Automatically filter and manage subscriptions
- **Outlook folder sync** — Classified emails are filed into your cloud folders automatically

---

## Meeting Intelligence

Capture and analyze every meeting. MyOpenBrain ingests transcripts from Zoom and Krisp, extracts key points, decisions, and action items, and links everything back to your workspace.

- **Automatic transcript ingestion** — Zoom and Krisp integration
- **Key point extraction** — AI identifies what matters from each meeting
- **Decision capture** — Decisions are logged with rationale, participants, and confidence scores
- **Action item flow** — Extracted tasks go straight to your Kanban board
- **Speaker identification** — Know who said what
- **Full-text search** — Find anything across your entire meeting history

---

## Kanban Boards

Flexible project management with drag-and-drop boards. Cards support due dates, priorities, checklists, tags, and direct links to the meetings and emails they came from.

- **Unlimited boards** — Organize by project, team, or workflow
- **Rich cards** — Due dates, priority levels, checklists, color tags, and descriptions
- **AI priority review** — Get AI suggestions on card prioritization
- **Email-linked cards** — Cards auto-created from emails carry an "Email" tag for traceability
- **Meeting-linked cards** — Action items from meetings link back to their source

---

## Pages & Workspaces

Organize knowledge into structured workspaces with rich pages. Each page has dedicated tabs for notes, knowledge entries, decisions, and smart rules — building a living knowledge base that grows automatically.

- **Block editor** — Rich text, markdown, slash commands, nested blocks
- **Knowledge tab** — Auto-populated knowledge entries from classified emails and meetings
- **Decisions tab** — Track decisions with status, category, and rationale
- **Smart rules** — Define plain-English rules per page to auto-capture relevant emails and meetings
- **Activity tab** — Page-specific activity history
- **Workspace hierarchy** — Group related pages together

---

## Smart Rules & Auto-Routing

Define rules in plain English and let AI route incoming content to the right places. Smart rules evaluate every new email and meeting, extract relevant content, tag items, and file them — closing the loop between incoming data and organized knowledge.

- **Natural language rules** — Write rules like "Anything related to Disney World vacation planning"
- **Auto-tagging** — Matched items get labeled with the page name
- **Content extraction** — AI extracts summaries, knowledge snippets, and decisions from matched items
- **Outlook folder filing** — Optionally move matched emails to linked cloud folders
- **Per-page configuration** — Each page can have its own smart rule and folder link
- **Confidence scoring** — Only high-confidence matches are routed (70%+ threshold)

## AI Daily Briefing

Start every day with an AI-generated summary of what matters. Your briefing includes overdue tasks, high-priority emails, today's meetings, and open action items — delivered to your inbox or viewed on your dashboard.

- **Automated daily email** — Arrives at your configured time each morning
- **Dashboard widget** — View your latest briefing anytime
- **Cross-source synthesis** — Combines Kanban, email, calendar, and action items
- **On-demand regeneration** — Refresh whenever you need an update

## Weekly Review

Get a comprehensive AI-generated review of your week. Topic clustering groups similar activities, cross-day patterns surface trends, and unresolved items are flagged for follow-up.

- **Automatic weekly synthesis** — AI reviews your entire week across all sources
- **Topic clustering** — Groups related activities together
- **Unresolved item alerts** — Flags incomplete action items and open decisions
- **Email delivery** — Receive your review via email

## Brain — Conversational AI Search

Ask questions across your entire work history. Brain searches meetings, emails, decisions, action items, and notes to give you sourced, contextual answers.

- **Natural language queries** — Ask questions like "What did we decide about the Q3 budget?"
- **Cross-source search** — Searches meetings, emails, pages, decisions, and action items
- **Source attribution** — Every answer cites its sources with context
- **Multi-session chat** — Maintain separate conversation threads by topic
- **Suggested prompts** — Get started quickly with template questions
